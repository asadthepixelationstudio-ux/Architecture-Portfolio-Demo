About Me

Professional Architect & Project Coordinator

With over 10 years of experience in architectural design and project coordination, I specialize in creating spaces that blend form and function seamlessly. My approach combines innovative design thinking with practical solutions to deliver projects that exceed client expectations.

I have worked on a diverse range of projects including residential, commercial, and institutional buildings, each with its unique challenges and opportunities.

My expertise spans from initial concept development and design to construction documentation and project management, ensuring a cohesive process from vision to reality.
